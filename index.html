Place must visit in Campus

School Auditorium

Our auditorium is very spacious, has a large seating capacity and has a wooden flored stage.

The School has two auditoriums, a seminar hall, two multipurpose halls and an amphitheater. A Beautiful Amphitheater is always bustling with activities like assemblies, dramatics and informal functions.

Sports Field

Our Sports Feild is very large. There many sports to play like cricket, Basketball, Horse Riding and ect.

The school has lush green grounds for cricket, hockey, football, basket ball , two lawn tennis clay courts, standard size skating rink and standard size horse riding area, one standard size badminton court, 10 mtr shooting range, chess room , table tennis room and MP hall for Karate.



achievments

Inter Amity Sports Competion
Inter Amity Sports Competion

More than 19000 students took part in the sixth edition of the Inter- Amity Sports Competition 2017-18, competing in 13 sports disciplines including skating, taekwondo, chess, kho-kho, etc. The school played host to competitions for aerobics.

Ramanujan Math Competion
Ramanujan Math Competion

School hosted 19th inter school Ramanujan competition of math in which more than 250 students from 29 eminent schools of Delhi / NCR participated. School team won the first prize in ‘Problem solving' for Class IX-X and ‘Math fun activity' for Class IV-V and the third prize in ‘Math Quiz' for Class VI-VIII.

Inter Amity Mental Math Quiz
Inter Amity Mental Math Quiz

School team won various laurels at 10th Inter Amity Mental Math quiz. It secured the first position in Class I, II, IV and V and third position in Class III. The school also hosted the finale for the students of Class X, participating in the annual quiz.

What's New

  • Almost from birth, infants learn, absorb information and explore the world around them.

    Amicots, a unique programme, is designed especially for infants between 9 and 14 months. It aims to provide high-quality child care, boost brain development and support developing skills in all domains. Amicots is an exceptional place where infants can thrive and advance comfortably and confidently towards understanding the world around them.

  • In Amiown’s Kindergarten, learning is all fun and games in an environment infused with happiness, joy, curiosity and excitement.

    A variety of unique play areas and innovative play techniques help the children learn and experience different things. Toddlers pick things up through an array of teacher guided activities that range from singing, dancing, storytelling, simple exercises, team activities, dramas and innovative play activities among others.
